Agatha Christie

Agatha Christie, the queen of crime fiction, was a British author born in 1890 and passed away in 1976. Her six-decade-long career produced 66 detective novels, numerous short stories, and successful plays. With iconic characters like Hercule Poirot and Miss Marple, Christie's intricate plots and clever twists kept readers guessing until the very end. Her meticulous attention to detail and deep understanding of human psychology set her works apart, making her one of the best-selling authors of all time. Christie's enduring legacy lies in her profound impact on the mystery genre, inspiring generations of authors to follow in her footsteps.

Jessica Watson

Jessica Watson is an Australian sailor born on May 18, 1993. She gained international recognition for her remarkable solo circumnavigation of the globe at the age of 16, becoming the youngest person to achieve this feat. Her journey aboard her yacht, Ella's Pink Lady, covered over 23,000 nautical miles and took 210 days to complete. Watson's determination, courage, and resilience captivated the world, inspiring young people to pursue their dreams. Her accomplishment earned her numerous accolades, including the Young Australian of the Year award in 2011. Jessica Watson's remarkable voyage continues to inspire and motivate individuals around the world.

Lady Gaga

Lady Gaga, born Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta on March 28, 1986, is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. Known for her eclectic style and boundary-pushing performances, Gaga has become one of the most influential and successful pop artists of her generation. With chart-topping hits like "Poker Face" and "Bad Romance," she has amassed a global fanbase. Gaga's artistry extends beyond music, as she has also ventured into acting, earning critical acclaim for her roles in films such as "A Star Is Born." She is an advocate for LGBTQ+ rights and uses her platform to promote inclusivity and acceptance. Lady Gaga's unique persona, powerful vocals, and fearless creativity have made her an icon in the entertainment industry.
